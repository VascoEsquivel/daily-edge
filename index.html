prediction engine
Model: regressed win% → log5 matchup probability + sport-specific home advantage (logit space). Soccer uses points-per-game strength with a closeness-weighted draw share; tournament play (World Cup knockouts) falls back to a market-based prior. Tennis uses ATP/WTA ranking-points share.
Vegas comparison uses ESPN's published line; "VALUE" flags games where the model disagrees with the market by ≥4%.
⭐ BEST PICK is the highest expected-value wager at the listed price (vig included; the unlisted dog price is estimated from a 4.5% book margin). Stake sizes are ¼-Kelly, in units of 1% of bankroll. ✋ NO BET means no side clears +2% EV — passing is a position.
🧠 The model learns: every final updates per-team Elo ratings and a per-sport calibration layer (stored locally in your browser). Predictions blend the record/ranking prior with learned Elo, and the Elo weight grows as the brain sees more of each team. ⭐ picks are logged at the pregame price and settled against the final score. 📜 History keeps a match-by-match record.
⚡ Live win probability uses the Stern model — current margin + time remaining vs the pregame expectation — and updates each poll; the ▲▼ shows movement vs the opening number. Click any upcoming match for its full bet board with model vs market prices on every side.
🎟️ The slip combines legs assuming independence; parlays stack the book's vig, so most are −EV — the slip will tell you. 💵 Set a bankroll to see stakes in dollars. Keys: ← → dates · t today · r refresh · 1–9 tabs.
