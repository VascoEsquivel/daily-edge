ℹ️ how DAILY EDGE works — the model, the math, the honest limits
Model: regressed win% → log5 matchup probability + sport-specific home advantage (logit space). Soccer uses points-per-game strength with a closeness-weighted draw share; tournament play (World Cup knockouts) falls back to a market-based prior. Tennis uses ATP/WTA ranking-points share. MLB factors the probable starters' ERA gap; team sports factor the live injury report weighted by player skill — an injured stat leader (⭐ in the 🏥 tooltip) counts 2-3x a rotation player, biggest impact in basketball. Day-to-day weighs less than out.
📐 Team strength blends the actual record with Pythagorean expectation from points scored/allowed (ESPN standings, all team sports). MLB additionally factors Baseball Savant Statcast expected-wOBA for hitting and pitching — contact-quality stats that lead results. See both in the expanded panel's form rows.
🎯 Totals: expected combined score from each team's scoring/allowing rates vs the posted O/U — Over/Under sides compete with moneyline/spread for the ⭐ best pick. MLB totals factor starters, bullpen relief ERA (MLB StatsAPI), game-time temperature/rain, and 💨 wind (Open-Meteo, computed as blowing-out/in relative to each park's center-field orientation — the Wrigley effect). Venue splits, 😴 back-to-back fatigue, and line movement vs the opener are all factored; "drivers" in the expanded panel says what's pushing each pick.
🎯 Player props: every expandable game card has a PLAYER PROPS section that pulls the real posted milestone lines, grades them against a season + last-10 game-log blend (🧲 anchored toward the listed price), and shows each player's L10 hit-rate meters. Honest limit: a season-rate model can't reliably out-sharp a prop market, so gaps are flagged to investigate, not as locks; props join parlays through the slip once you've vetted them.
🎯 Player Prop Lab: a contextual projection engine — starts from the season rate, blends recent form (with a role/usage-change detector when recent diverges ≥20% from season), then scales by matchup and pace. Projects the over/under curve (Poisson for counting stats, Normal for high-count) and prints an alternate-line ladder with fair Kalshi prices in cents, built for spotting mispriced alt lines. Enter a price to grade the bet and add it to your slip. Matchup/pace are your read (or a defense-vs-stat rank); still a shortlist tool, not gospel.
🔥🥶 Hot/cold: recent margin form gives a small, capped model shift (streaks are mostly noise — never more than ~3%) and chips flag teams 4-1 or better / 1-4 or worse in their last five. Player-level streaks aren't in free data feeds; prop angles show season rates.
Vegas comparison uses ESPN's published line; "VALUE" flags games where the model disagrees with the market by ≥4%.
⭐ BEST PICK is the highest expected-value wager at the listed price (vig included; the unlisted dog price is estimated from a 4.5% book margin). Stake sizes are ¼-Kelly, in units of 1% of bankroll. ✋ NO BET means no side clears +2% EV — passing is a position.
🧭 TOP-DOWN BAYESIAN CORE: the de-vigged market line is the PRIOR — the sharpest public estimate there is. The whole bottom-up stack (Glicko-deviation Elo, Pythagorean, Statcast, park & wind, injuries, fatigue, form) computes a likelihood that may nudge that prior by a hard-clamped ±5 points (the α you see on every pick strip). Posted spreads and totals are treated as the market's median: the sim only supplies a bounded α around 50/50. Rogue data is mathematically unable to manufacture a giant fake edge. ⚖️ Stakes are portfolio Kelly: the day's picks are priced as one correlated book (same game ρ=.35, same sport .12, cross-sport .05) and every stake scales down together whenever correlated exposure would top 12u.
🧠 The model learns: every final updates per-team Elo ratings (margin-of-victory weighted, faster for barely-seen teams) and a per-sport calibration layer (stored locally in your browser). On launch it studies the last 45 days of results in chronological order, then stays current. Predictions blend the record/ranking prior with learned Elo, and the Elo weight grows as the brain sees more of each team. ⭐ picks are logged at the pregame price and settled against the final score. The brain bar separates two records: model picks · straight-up (how often the who-wins pick is right) and ⭐ suggested-bet hit (how often the +EV plays cash — usually lower, since value bets are often plus-money dogs, with ROI showing they still profit). "exp" is the rate the model expected; beating it means the edge is real. 📜 History keeps a match-by-match record. ⬆⬇ Export/import moves a trained brain between devices.
⚡ Live win probability uses the Stern model — current margin + time remaining vs the pregame expectation — and updates each poll; the ▲▼ shows movement vs the opening number. Click any upcoming match for its full bet board with model vs market prices on every side.
